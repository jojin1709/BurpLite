Privacy Policy

BurpLite is committed to preserving the privacy of its users. This extension does not collect, record, track, transmit, or share any personal data, user credentials, browsing history, telemetry, or network traffic logs.

All core capabilities (including request tampering, fuzzing, encoding/decoding, and security scanning) operate entirely locally within your browser's DevTools execution context. No network requests are made to external backend servers except those initiated by the user through the Repeater, Intruder, or Collaborator configurations.

By using BurpLite, you agree that your actions and traffic are processed strictly locally on your own client machine.
